Williams Roofing brings over 15 years of hands-on roofing experience to every project in Houston. As a family-run, owner-operated business, we combine old-school craftsmanship with modern roofing technology to deliver honest, transparent service you can trust.

Family Legacy

Honest Roofing for Houston Families
Our Mission:

Williams Roofing was born from a simple observation: Houston needed more honest, reliable roofers. My grandfather was a roofer, my father was a roofer, and I grew up watching both of them build trust one shingle at a time. When I started Williams Roofing in 2011, I wanted to carry that legacy forward—offering the same integrity and craftsmanship that defined their work, with the transparency and communication today's homeowners deserve.

We've built our reputation on owner accountability. When you call Williams Roofing, you're not handed off to a sales team or subcontractors—you work directly with Will from estimate through installation. That means every detail, every material choice, and every timeline is managed by someone who has skin in the game and who will still be here years from now when you need maintenance or a warranty call.

Over 15 years and 450+ satisfied customers later, our mission remains the same: deliver top-tier roofing with upfront pricing, clear timelines, and a 1-year warranty you can count on. Whether it's a full roof replacement after storm damage, a repair to extend your roof's life, or a code-compliant exhaust installation, we treat every project like it's our own home. That's the Williams Roofing difference.

We treat every roof like it's protecting our own family. That's the standard my grandfather set, and it's the standard we keep today.

What Drives Us Every Day
Our Core Values:

01

Transparent Pricing

We tell you exactly what your roof needs—no upselling, no hidden fees. Every estimate includes a detailed breakdown of materials, labor, and timeline. You'll know the final price before we start, and that price won't change unless you request additional work.

02

Owner Accountability

When you hire Williams Roofing, you work directly with Will—the owner. From your initial call through project completion, you'll have one point of contact who understands your property, your budget, and your concerns. No sales reps, no runaround.

03

Clear Communication

We show up on time, return calls promptly, and keep you updated throughout the project. If weather delays installation or we encounter unexpected damage during tear-off, you'll know immediately. Open communication is how we've earned 450+ five-star reviews.

04

Generational Expertise

Three generations of roofing experience means we've seen every type of roof system, every failure mode, and every installation challenge. We bring that knowledge to your project, whether it's a straightforward shingle replacement or a complex multi-plane design.

05

1-Year Warranty

Every Williams Roofing installation comes with a 1-year workmanship warranty covering materials and installation quality. If an issue arises due to our work, we'll return to fix it at no charge. We also help you register manufacturer warranties for long-term protection.

06

Licensed & Insured

Williams Roofing is fully licensed and insured for residential and commercial roofing in Texas. We carry general liability and workers' compensation coverage, ensuring you're protected if the unexpected happens during your project.
